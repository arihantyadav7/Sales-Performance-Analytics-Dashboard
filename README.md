# Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes business sales data using Power BI to identify sales trends, top-performing products, regional performance, and category-wise profitability.

## Objective

* Analyze sales performance
* Identify top-selling products
* Compare regional sales
* Generate business insights and recommendations
* Build an interactive dashboard

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Microsoft Power BI

## Dataset

* Superstore Sales Dataset

## Dashboard Features

* Total Sales KPI
* Total Profit KPI
* Total Orders KPI
* Total Quantity KPI
* Sales Trend Over Time
* Top Products by Sales
* Sales by Region
* Profit by Category
* Interactive Slicers

## Key Insights

* Sales show a positive growth trend over time.
* Technology category contributes significantly to revenue.
* Top products generate a major share of total sales.
* Regional analysis helps identify high-performing markets.

## Recommendations

* Focus on high-performing categories.
* Improve inventory management for top-selling products.
* Optimize pricing and discount strategies.
* Increase marketing efforts in strong-performing regions.
